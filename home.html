Hi! I am an associate researcher at the Spatial Lab in ShenZhen University. I recieved my Ph.D degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Xiaoru Yuan. I was a member of Visualization and Visual Analytics Group of Peking University.
I have done several projects in a series of trajectory analysis methods and some new projects about agile visualization and interactions, such as Interaction+. My research interest is general visualization methodology and visual analytics in the context of Urban Informatics.
