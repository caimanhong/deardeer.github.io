Min Lu

Hi! I am now working as an assistant professor at Shenzhen University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Xiaoru Yuan, Visualization and Visual Analytics Group of Peking University.

My research interest is generally Visualization and Visual Analytics, including ♥ propose intuitive and efficient visualization to represent or explore data (e.g., Winglets, Interaction+.); ♥ apply machine learning method to understand visual design (e.g., VIF); ♥ develop visual analytical system to analyze urban problems (e.g., a series of trajectory analysis methods).
